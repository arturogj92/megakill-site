# MEGAKILL 🔫

**Force-quit your Mac apps by shooting them with a shotgun.**

![MEGAKILL — shooting apps in the Dock and on the desktop](assets/megakill-demo.gif)

Hold a shortcut and your cursor turns into a shotgun. Aim at any app — its Dock icon or its window — pull the trigger, and it gets force-quit in a burst of pixels, sparks and a satisfying BOOM. Flying shell casings, screen shake, and DOOM-style kill-streak callouts.

- Free to try — 100 kills, then unlock unlimited.
- Lives in your menu bar. macOS 14+. Notarized by Apple.
