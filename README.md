# MEGAKILL 🔫

**Force-quit your Mac apps by shooting them.**

![MEGAKILL: shooting apps in the Dock and on the desktop](assets/megakill-demo.gif)

MEGAKILL is a macOS menu-bar app that turns force-quitting into a one-click shotgun blast. Hold a shortcut and your cursor becomes a DOOM-style shotgun. Aim at any app, its Dock icon or its window, pull the trigger, and it gets force-quit on the spot with flying shell casings, screen shake, real shotgun sounds, and kill-streak callouts.

## What it does

- **Shoot to force-quit.** Click a frozen app's Dock icon or window and it dies instantly. Right-click reloads (two shots per reload).
- **Wound mode.** A shot on a single window closes just that window and leaves the app running. A shot on the Dock icon always kills the whole app.
- **Pinned cleanup.** Shoot a closed-but-pinned Dock app and it gets unpinned in one shot.
- **Close floating pop-ups.** Blast a nagging alert window (like an Outlook reminder) to dismiss it.
- **Kill streaks, screen shake, real shotgun sounds** (sound has an on/off toggle), plus launch at login.
- **Safe by design.** Finder, the Dock, and critical system processes are shielded, so you cannot break your Mac.
