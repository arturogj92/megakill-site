How it works — 3 shots

Quitting apps shouldn't be boring.

01 SUMMON

Hit the shortcut

Hold your modifier combo (⌃⌥⌘ by default). The cursor turns into a shotgun over any screen. You're armed.

02 AIM

Pick your target

Aim at the app you want gone — its Dock icon or right at its window. That browser with 38 tabs that's been open for three weeks eating your RAM.

03 EXECUTE

Fire

BOOM. Instant real force-quit, the shotgun blast roars, and if it was pinned it gets unpinned: the icon vanishes from the Dock for good.

DOOM shotgun

Real sounds

Authentic fire and reload, not system beeps. Every kill is heard. Your office mates will know.

Configurable shortcut

Summon it with your modifier combo. From the urge to kill to force-quit in under a second, no menus.

Safety guard

Finder, the Dock and MEGAKILL are untouchable. It only shoots running apps; critical system processes are shielded.
